About us

We are a Tech-consulting firm with a specialization in FinTech industry; providing one-stop solution for all your financial problems. Sowing the seeds in July 2017, our company has now matured into a big tree. We have provided solutions for various nuanced issues in various companies across India and abroad. From implementing UPI for a leading private sector bank of India to reducing loss rates for a US-based lending firm, we have completed all of our projects within a year, because we understand the money-value of time. We provide tailored solutions to your specific concerns. Our services include suggesting various solutions to your problems and aiding in executing the desired solution.

Our Services

We aim to empower businesses in the fintech sector to strategize, build, and leverage evolving technologies to navigate through the digital transformation and create market-ready solutions.

We identify patterns, opportunities, and threats to your business while building managed dashboards, applications, and algorithms to make sense of your customer data.

We provide tailored data-driven solutions to your specific problems to attain quantified achievable targets in a given time-frame.
